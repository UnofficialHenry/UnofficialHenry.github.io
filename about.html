About

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe — a simple browser game you can play instantly. The goal is to get three in a row before the computer does.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Ads may be displayed to help support hosting and development.

How to play

  • Click Play to start a new game.
  • You play as X. The computer plays as O.
  • Get three in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win.
  • If the board fills and nobody wins, it’s a draw.

Tech notes

The game runs entirely in your browser. There’s no login and no account required. For details about advertising and cookies, see the Privacy Policy.
